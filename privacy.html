Mountain State Brewing Co. Internal Tools

This application is an internal tool used exclusively by Mountain State Brewing Co. It connects to QuickBooks Online via OAuth 2.0 to create sales orders.

Data we access: QuickBooks Online accounting data (customers, products, sales orders) for Mountain State Brewing Co. only.

Data we store: We do not store any QuickBooks data. OAuth tokens are held temporarily in your browser session only.

Third parties: No data is shared with any third parties.
